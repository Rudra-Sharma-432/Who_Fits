Who Fits

ABOUT THE GAME

Who Fits is a social party game where players vote for who they think best matches a given phrase. There are no right or wrong answers — the fun comes from how your friends see each other and reacting to the results together.

👥 2–10 Players 🎲 Party Game 😂 Social & Chaotic

HOW TO PLAY

1

Join a Room

One player creates a room and shares the Room ID with friends. Others can join from the public list or enter a room code directly.

2

A Phrase Appears

Every round, a prompt is shown to all players — something like "Most likely to survive a zombie apocalypse" or "Talks non-stop".

3

Vote!

Vote for the player you think fits the phrase best. You can vote for yourself. The host controls how many votes each player gets per round.

4

See the Results

After everyone votes, the results are revealed — showing who got how many votes and exactly who voted for whom (unless anonymous mode is on). React, laugh, and discuss!

💡 TIPS

Play on voice chat — reactions during the reveal are the best part.

There are no right answers, so vote honestly — it's more fun that way.

Turn on anonymous mode if you want chaos — no one knows who voted for whom!
